# BloomHer
A modern Women Wellness Website designed to support physical, mental, and emotional wellbeing in one place. It features a personalized dashboard, fitness and nutrition guidance, mood and period tracking, and a calming, user-friendly design to help women live healthier and more balanced lives.
The Women Wellness Website is a modern, user-friendly, and empowering digital platform designed to support women in achieving a healthy and balanced lifestyle. It brings together essential aspects of wellbeing—including physical health, mental wellness, emotional care, fitness, and nutrition—into one beautifully designed and accessible space.

The platform features a calming and elegant user interface built with soft pastel colors, smooth animations, and intuitive navigation to create a relaxing and engaging experience. It is fully responsive, ensuring seamless access across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

At the core of the website is a personalized user dashboard, where users can track daily wellness activities such as water intake, sleep, mood, and fitness progress. It also includes a period tracker, helping women manage their health more effectively.

To enhance user support, the website offers an AI-powered wellness assistant that provides guidance on mental health, fitness routines, and lifestyle improvements. Additional features include guided yoga sessions, workout plans, nutrition advice, journaling tools, and meditation resources, all aimed at promoting holistic wellbeing.

The platform also includes a safe and supportive community space, where users can share experiences, seek advice, and connect with others anonymously. Safety features such as an SOS alert option further add to its usefulness.

Overall, the Women Wellness Website is more than just a health platform—it is a complete digital companion that empowers women to take control of their wellbeing, build healthy habits, and lead a confident and fulfilling life
