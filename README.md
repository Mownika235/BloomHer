# BloomHer
A modern Women Wellness Website designed to support physical, mental, and emotional wellbeing in one place. It features a personalized dashboard, fitness and nutrition guidance, mood and period tracking, and a calming, user-friendly design to help women live healthier and more balanced lives.
 STEP 1:Homepage Experience
The user lands on a visually attractive homepage with soft pastel colors, a motivational tagline, and easy navigation options like Home, Dashboard, Features, and Contact.
 STEP 2:User Registration & Login
Users can create an account or log in securely to access personalized wellness features.
 STEP 3:Personal Dashboard
After login, users enter a dashboard that shows their daily wellness summary including water intake, sleep, steps, and overall health progress.
 STEP 4:Health & Mood Tracking
Users can track their mood using simple emoji inputs and monitor their emotional wellbeing daily.
 STEP 5:Period Tracker
A built-in calendar helps users track menstrual cycles, predict upcoming dates, and manage health effectively.
 STEP 5:Fitness & Yoga Section
Provides guided workouts and yoga routines suitable for different fitness levels.
 STEP 6:Nutrition & Diet Guidance
Offers healthy meal suggestions and diet plans tailored for different health goals.
 STEP 7:Mental Wellness Support
Includes journaling, meditation guides, and stress-relief activities for better mental health.
 STEP 8:AI Wellness Assistant
A chatbot provides instant support, tips, and personalized suggestions for health and lifestyle.
 STEP 9:Community & Support
Users can interact, share experiences, and seek advice in a safe and supportive environment.
 STEP 10:Safety Features
Includes an SOS alert option and emergency support features for user safety.
 STEP 11:Responsive & User-Friendly Design
The website works smoothly on all devices with simple navigation, soft visuals, and an engaging user experience.
